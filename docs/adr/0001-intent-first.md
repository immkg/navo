# ADR 0001: Intent First

**Status:** Accepted

## Context

Traditional productivity tools begin with tasks.

People rarely think this way.

They usually begin with an outcome they want to achieve, while the work required to reach that outcome is discovered over time.

## Decision

Navo will model Intent as the highest-level concept.

Every piece of work exists because it advances one or more intentions.

Tasks are therefore one representation of work rather than the foundation of the system.

## Consequences

### Benefits

- Models real-world thinking.
- Allows planning to evolve naturally.
- Supports multiple execution strategies.
- Separates goals from implementation.

### Trade-offs

- More complex than task-based systems.
- Requires stronger relationships between domain objects.
