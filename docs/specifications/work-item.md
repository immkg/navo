# Work Item Specification

## Purpose

A Work Item represents one unit of meaningful work.

It is the primary execution object in Navo.

## Responsibilities

A Work Item:

- advances one or more Intentions
- participates in relationships
- carries execution details
- supports collaboration

## Types

- Task
- Decision
- Meeting
- Reminder
- Purchase
- Approval
- Research
- Review
- Milestone
- Custom

## Rules

A Work Item:

- has a unique identity
- may belong to multiple Intentions
- may depend on other Work Items
- may have multiple participants
- may support multiple locations
- may be unscheduled

## Relationships

A Work Item may:

- depend on
- block
- create
- reference
- replace
- relate to

other Work Items.
