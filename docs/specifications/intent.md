# Intent Specification

## Purpose

An Intent represents a desired outcome.

It is the highest-level object in Navo and answers one question:

> What is the user trying to achieve?

## Responsibilities

An Intent:

* defines an outcome
* groups related work
* tracks overall progress
* remains stable as work changes

## Rules

* An Intent may contain zero or more Work Items.
* An Intent may depend on other Intents.
* An Intent may be collaborative.
* An Intent does not define execution order.
* An Intent does not define a schedule.

## Relationships

Intent

* contains Work Items
* may reference other Intents
* may have participants
* may have context

## Examples

* Plan a vacation
* Organize a wedding
* Buy groceries
* Prepare for interviews
