# Planning

Planning is the process of choosing a path toward an intention.

Unlike traditional planning systems, Navo treats planning as an ongoing activity rather than a one-time event.

Plans evolve as work progresses.

New information creates new opportunities.

Completed work unlocks additional work.

Planning is continuous because life is continuously changing.

The goal of planning is not to produce a perfect checklist.

The goal is to continuously answer one question:

> What is the best path forward?
