# Work

Work is anything that moves an intent forward.

Unlike traditional task managers, Navo does not assume that all work is a task.

Examples of work include:

- Complete a task
- Make a decision
- Conduct research
- Attend a meeting
- Purchase an item
- Review a document
- Approve a request
- Wait for someone else

Work is the fundamental unit of execution.

Every piece of work exists because it contributes toward one or more intentions.

## Characteristics

Work may:

- depend on other work
- create new work
- involve multiple people
- happen at one or many locations
- be optional or required
- change over time

Work is connected.

Understanding those relationships is more important than managing isolated tasks.
